Biography

Embarking on the path to becoming an esthetician, I've cultivated a passion for skincare and beauty. My journey started with formal education in cosmetics and esthetics, where I honed my skills in various skincare treatments and techniques. Dedicated to staying current with industry trends, I attended workshops and sought hands-on experience to refine my craft. Building a foundation of knowledge in skin and product ingredients, I aim to provide personalized skincare solutions. Committed to creating a positive client experience, I blend creativity with technical expertise in pursuit of a fulfilling career as an esthetician.

Qualifications

  • Certification in Makeup Artistry - Training in Bridal Makeup - Specialized Courses in Special Effects or Theatrical Makeup
  • State-issued Esthetician License- 1 - 1/2 years
  • Certification in Advanced Skincare Techniques - Training in Chemical Peels, Microdermabrasion, and dermaplanning
  • Certification in Professional Waxing - Expertise in Brazilian Waxing Techniques - Training in Speed Waxing
  • Certification in Lash Extensions
