Demi Williams

Biography

With nearly a decade in esthetics, my journey began as a regional trainer at European Wax Center, where I honed my craft and nurtured teams. Following this, I found solace in the holistic spa world of Falls Church, delving deeper into natural skincare.

Throughout my five years in spas, I explored various avenues, including permanent cosmetics and lash extensions. However, my true passion lies in facials and waxing, where I've been praised for my "magical touch."

My personal struggle with skincare during college drove me to transition from accounting to esthetics. Now, my mission is simple: to help others find beauty and confidence in their own skin.

Qualifications

  • Certified in Professional Hard wax- speed waxing, brazilian and guyzilan expertise
  • Certification in make up theory and practical
  • Certified in lash extensions, brow lamination/tint
